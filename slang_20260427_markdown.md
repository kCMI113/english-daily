# Slang Glossary

> Converted from the uploaded PDF handout.

## A

### Addy
**Meaning:** Abbreviation for **address**.  
**Example:** I'll meet you there, just send me the addy.

### Amirite
**Meaning:** An informal way to say “am I right,” used to seek agreement after making a statement.  
**Example:** That taco was bomb, amirite?

### Amped
**Meaning:** Excited.  
**Example:** I'm amped for the concert tonight!

### Aura
**Meaning:** Used to compliment someone's ability, perceived power, or sense of coolness and mystery. It became popular in soccer and basketball communities on Twitter in 2023, so it is often used to compliment athletes.  
**Example:** Did you see Steph Curry's clutch shot last night? He's got crazy aura.

## B

### Bae
**Meaning:** Boyfriend or girlfriend.  
**Example:** Wait, bae is calling me.

### Bandwagon
**Meaning:** Someone who takes an interest in something just to fit in with the crowd.  
**Example:** Maggie suddenly loves rugby; she is a major bandwagon fan.

### Basic
**Meaning:** Someone who is unoriginal and only interested in popular, trending things.  
**Example:** She is so basic with her pumpkin spice latte.

### Bed rot
**Meaning:** Spending extended periods of time in bed, often engaging in activities like watching TV or reading.  
**Example:** After a hectic week, I'm looking forward to some much-needed bed rot this weekend.

### Bet
**Meaning:** In agreement with something, or used for okay or affirmative.  
**Example:**  
Person 1: Want to come over to my house?  
Person 2: Bet!

### Boomer / Okay Boomer
**Meaning:** A dismissive retort used to mock the opinions, ideas, or attitudes of older individuals, particularly members of the Baby Boomer generation.  
**Example:** When Clayton's great-uncle started complaining about millennials' work ethic, he responded with, “Okay, Boomer.”

### Boujee
**Meaning:** Rich, luxurious, fancy.  
**Example:** You are not going to this boujee country club?

### Bussin’
**Meaning:** Something is really good.  
**Example:** This burrito is bussin’! I'm definitely coming back here.

## C

### Cap
**Meaning:** Lying or faking.  
**Example:** I actually went to class today. No cap.

### Cash
**Meaning:** Awesome or cool.  
**Example:** That new movie is so cash!

### Catch feels
**Meaning:** Develop romantic or emotional attachment to someone.  
**Example:** I noticed Lea starting to catch feels for Bruce.

### Caught in 4k
**Meaning:** Someone caught in the act of something compromising.  
**Example:** He thought he could get away with lying, but we have him caught in 4k.

### Chat
**Meaning:** Used to refer to a group of people, like friends or people in their class.  
**Example:** Chat, do you think he's telling the truth?

### Cheugy
**Meaning:** Someone or something that is out-of-date, unfashionable, or trying too hard.  
**Example:** I can't believe she's still wearing skinny jeans, that's so cheugy.

### Cooked
**Meaning:** Something or someone that is done, exhausted, or doomed.  
**Example:** I was up all night but didn't even get halfway through the study guide. I'm cooked.

### Cringe
**Meaning:** A feeling of embarrassment and discomfort.  
**Example:** Watching Annie try to talk to James was so cringe.

## D

### Dank
**Meaning:** Excellent, high quality.  
**Example:** The party last night was dank!

### Dip
**Meaning:** To leave suddenly or early.  
**Example:** I'm about to dip.

### DL
**Meaning:** An acronym that stands for **down low**, referring to an activity or information that is meant to be kept discreet.  
**Example:** We've been talking, but we're keeping it on the DL for now.

### Dope
**Meaning:** Something that is exceptional or awesome.  
**Example:** That bike is dope!

### Dox
**Meaning:** To seek out and publish private information online about someone, usually with malicious intentions.  
**Example:** Jennifer got doxed after getting in a fight with someone during an online game. Now her address and phone number are all over social media.

### Drag
**Meaning:** To mock or humiliate someone.  
**Example:** Bettie will never be invited back after she dragged Peter.

### Drip
**Meaning:** Sophisticated or stylish in appearance.  
**Example:** You got drip with those new shoes.

### Dub
**Meaning:** Short for **W**, meaning win or winner.  
**Example:** We took the dub.

## E

### Egirl / Eboy
**Meaning:** Frequently found on TikTok; someone who has adopted an edgy style including a blushed nose, heavy eyeliner, and little hearts under the eyes.  
**Example:** I've been thinking about changing my style to something more like an Eboy.

### Extra
**Meaning:** Attention grabbing or dramatic.  
**Example:** I didn't invite her, she's too extra.

## F

### Facts
**Meaning:** Emphasizes agreement with something that is accurate or true.  
**Example:**  
Person 1: His new album is fire.  
Person 2: Straight facts!

### Faded
**Meaning:** Being under the influence of marijuana.  
**Example:** You should have seen her last night, she was so faded.

### Fan service
**Meaning:** Elements added to an entertainment piece that are a subtle nod to avid fans, like an inside joke that other viewers won't understand. Also known as easter eggs.  
**Example:** If you're a Marvel fan you'll love this episode. It has a ton of fan service.

### Finna
**Meaning:** Abbreviation for **fixing to**, or going to.  
**Example:** I'm finna go to the mall.

### Finsta
**Meaning:** A combination of the words “fake” and “Instagram.” Refers to a secondary, private Instagram account that users create to share content with a smaller, more selective audience.  
**Example:** She posted silly selfies and behind-the-scenes moments on her finsta for her close friends to see.

### Fire
**Meaning:** Amazing or exciting.  
**Example:** That waterslide is fire!

### Fit
**Meaning:** An outfit.  
**Example:** You're always so fashionable. Amazing fit!

### Flex
**Meaning:** To flaunt or brag about something.  
**Example:** He's flexing with those kicks.

### FR
**Meaning:** An acronym that means **for real**, used to agree with someone.  
**Example:**  
Person 1: The homework was so hard.  
Person 2: FR.

### FRFR
**Meaning:** Abbreviation for **for real for real**. A way to emphasize sincerity or seriousness.  
**Example:** I'm tired of waiting for you, the mall is always the same. FRFR!

### FYP
**Meaning:** Refers to TikTok's “For You Page” that is filled with recommended content and videos personalized for the viewer.  
**Example:** My FYP had the weirdest feed today.

## G

### Gassing
**Meaning:** Exaggerating someone else's abilities, accomplishments, or qualities.  
**Example:** She's already confident enough; she doesn't need any more gassing.

### Ghost
**Meaning:** Cutting off communication with someone with no explanation.  
**Example:** We went on a date, and then he ghosted me.

### Giving me life
**Meaning:** Something or someone that is exciting.  
**Example:** This warm weather is giving me life!

### Glow-up
**Meaning:** A major transformation in appearance in a way that is generally perceived as attractive.  
**Example:** She had a glow-up. She looks great!

### GOAT
**Meaning:** An acronym that stands for **greatest of all time**, praising someone for being the best. A variation is “goated.”  
**Example:** My coach is the GOAT!

### Granola
**Meaning:** A person who is environmentally aware and enjoys the outdoors.  
**Example:** You'll probably run into her on a hike. She's granola.

### Guap
**Meaning:** A large sum of money.  
**Example:** Sally must have paid a lot of guap for those shoes.

### Gucci
**Meaning:** Fancy, fashionable, or excellent.  
**Example:**  
Person 1: I'll pick you up in 10.  
Person 2: Gucci.

## H

### Hammered
**Meaning:** Extremely intoxicated from alcohol or drugs.  
**Example:** He was so hammered we had to take him to the E.R.

### Heated
**Meaning:** Angry or frustrated.  
**Example:** He got heated when I told him I took his bill.

### Here for this
**Meaning:** Excited about something; showing support.  
**Example:** You're entering your boss era. I'm here for this.

### High key
**Meaning:** Something that is overt, obvious, or done with a high level of intensity.  
**Example:** I high key love that new movie.

### Hits different
**Meaning:** Something that is special, unique, or causes a strong emotional reaction that is difficult to articulate.  
**Example:** My mom's cooking just hits different.

### Hollywood
**Meaning:** A negative change in behavior in someone who achieves success and looks down on their past life.  
**Example:** After Alice made the cheerleading team she became Hollywood.

### Hop off
**Meaning:** An expression used to tell someone to stop bothering or annoying another.  
**Example:** When he kept pestering her with questions, she told him to hop off and give her some space.

### Hot take
**Meaning:** A strongly worded and often controversial opinion on a current event or popular topic.  
**Example:** I watched the new superhero movie. The cast was amazing, but the storyline was sketch. That's my hot take for the day.

### Hype
**Meaning:** Excited, or something is good.  
**Example:** This song is so hype!

## I

### Ick
**Meaning:** A feeling of disgust towards someone or something.  
**Example:** Did you see how mean he was to his little brother? That's such an ick.

### ICYMI
**Meaning:** An acronym that stands for **in case you missed it**.  
**Example:** ICYMI, I'm headed to IU State this fall.

### IRL
**Meaning:** An acronym that stands for **in real life**. Used to distinguish between online and offline interactions.  
**Example:** He has a lot of online friends, but he's never met them IRL.

### IYKYK
**Meaning:** An acronym that stands for **if you know, you know**. Indicates insider knowledge or a shared experience between a group of people.  
**Example:** The ski trip was wild! IYKYK.

## J

### Juul
**Meaning:** A brand of electronic cigarettes, also known as a vape.  
**Example:** She discreetly pulled out her Juul and took a puff while waiting for the bus.

## K

### Keep it 100
**Meaning:** To be honest, genuine, and authentic in all situations.  
**Example:** I appreciate when my friends keep it 100 with me, even if the truth might be hard to hear.

## L

### L
**Meaning:** Loss, loser.  
**Example:** He took the L.

### Left on read
**Meaning:** When someone has read a message sent to them, but has not responded to it.  
**Example:** I texted him last night, but he left me on read. I'm not sure if he's busy or just ignoring me.

### Lit
**Meaning:** Exciting, excellent, or intoxicated.  
**Example:** The movie was lit!

### LMAO
**Meaning:** An acronym that stands for **laughing my a** off**; indicates something is funny.  
**Example:** Remember when I fell going up the stairs? LMAO!

### LMS
**Meaning:** An acronym that stands for **like my status**, used on social media asking viewers to like the content.  
**Example:** LMS to enter the giveaway.

### LOL
**Meaning:** An acronym that stands for **laugh out loud**, expressing amusement at something.  
**Example:** LOL at your ostrich video!

## M

### Mad
**Meaning:** Really, extremely, or crazy.  
**Example:** It's mad hot outside.

### Mid
**Meaning:** Average or not great.  
**Example:** That show was mid.

## N

### Netflix and chill
**Meaning:** A euphemism for hooking up.  
**Example:** I asked Ricky if he wanted to Netflix and chill, and he actually came ready with popcorn. lol

### NGL
**Meaning:** An acronym that stands for **not gonna lie**, admitting something honestly and openly.  
**Example:** A lot of people didn't like the movie, but ngl, I loved it.

### NSFW
**Meaning:** An acronym that stands for **not safe for work**. Used to label content, such as images, videos, or links, that may contain explicit or inappropriate material not suitable for viewing in a professional or public setting.  
**Example:** I can't open that link right now. It's marked as NSFW, and I'm at school.

## O

### OMG
**Meaning:** An acronym that stands for **oh my gosh/God**, indicating something is shocking or surprising.  
**Example:** OMG, I can't believe you just said that!

### OML
**Meaning:** An acronym that stands for **oh my Lord**, an exclamation of surprise or excitement.  
**Example:** OML, you scared me!

### OMW
**Meaning:** An acronym that stands for **on my way**, a way to indicate a person is en route.  
**Example:** OMW! I'll be there in 20.

### ONG
**Meaning:** **On God**, a way to show strong agreement with something, similar to “I swear to God.”  
**Example:** I just saw them kissing! ONG.

### On fleek
**Meaning:** Perfectly done, exactly right, excellent.  
**Example:** Your makeup is on fleek.

### On point
**Meaning:** Perfect, exactly right.  
**Example:** His outfit is on point.

### Only in Ohio
**Meaning:** Used to label things that are strange or cursed.  
**Example:** Why are there rats on the train? Only in Ohio.

### OTP
**Meaning:** An acronym that stands for **one true pairing**. Refers to a favorite or ideal romantic pairing between characters in a book, movie, TV show, or video game.  
**Example:** I've shipped this OTP since the pilot episode. They have the best chemistry on screen.

## P

### Periodt
**Meaning:** Emphasizes the finality and decisiveness of a statement.  
**Example:** He's the best dancer in the group, periodt.

### Pressed
**Meaning:** Feeling annoyed or stressed about something.  
**Example:** Why are you so pressed?

### Pulling
**Meaning:** To attract attention from romantic interests.  
**Example:** I saw him pull at least five girls at the party.

### Put on blast
**Meaning:** To embarrass someone publicly over social media by denouncing or exposing them.  
**Example:** He was put on blast for breaking up with her over a text.

## R

### Rad
**Meaning:** Awesome, or very good.  
**Example:** This view is rad.

### Real
**Meaning:** Another way to say “serious.” It is often used as part of other phrases like “the real real.”  
**Example:** You think you can pull off that dress? Be so for real.

### Receipts
**Meaning:** Evidence or proof of someone's actions or words, often used to support a claim.  
**Example:** When she said I was lying, he came prepared with receipts, showing screenshots of their conversations to prove his point.

### Rent free
**Meaning:** Obsessing over someone or something; living inside someone's head.  
**Example:** What he said to me still lives in my head rent free.

### Rizz
**Meaning:** The ability to seduce or charm a potential love interest.  
**Example:** That boy has so much rizz. How's he been single since elementary school?

### RN
**Meaning:** Abbreviation for **right now**. Indicates something that's happening at the present moment.  
**Example:** I'm busy rn, I'll call you later.

## S

### Salty
**Meaning:** Upset, resentful, or irritated.  
**Example:** He was salty after losing, so I told him to leave the game.

### Savage
**Meaning:** A fierce or bold person.  
**Example:** He's savage on the court.

### Say less
**Meaning:** Agreement or understanding, so no further explanation is needed because the point has been understood.  
**Example:** When she suggested going to their favorite restaurant, he simply nodded and said, “Say less.”

### Sending me
**Meaning:** Response to a funny text message, expressing extreme amusement.  
**Example:** lollll that panda meme is sending me!

### Shade
**Meaning:** Subtle contempt or disgust for someone.  
**Example:** Did she just throw shade at me?

### Ship
**Meaning:** To support the romantic relationship between two people.  
**Example:** I totally ship Timothée Chalamet and Kylie Jenner.

### Shook
**Meaning:** Shocked or surprised.  
**Example:** She actually did the assignment. I'm shook!

### Shorty
**Meaning:** An attractive girl.  
**Example:** Hey, shorty! Let's grab a drink.

### Sick
**Meaning:** Cool or awesome.  
**Example:** Did you see that tackle? That was sick!

### Simp
**Meaning:** Someone who is desperate for affection or romantic relationships.  
**Example:** Dude, you're being such a simp.

### Skibidi Toilet
**Meaning:** An animated YouTube series that depicts a dystopian world where mobile toilets with human heads popping out of their bowls war with humanoids who have cameras, speakers, or TVs for heads. By 2024, it became popular with Gen Alpha and the youngest portion of Gen Z.  

### Slap
**Meaning:** Amazing or excellent.  
**Example:** This song slaps!

### Slay
**Meaning:** Doing something extremely well.  
**Example:** You slayed your dance recital!

### SMH
**Meaning:** Abbreviation for **shaking my head**. Used when there are no words to describe something stupid.  
**Example:** I warned Zane he didn't have enough time to shower before his flight. I was right, he missed it. SMH.

### SO
**Meaning:** An acronym that stands for **shout out**, a public expression of greeting, praise, or acknowledgement directed towards a person. It is also known for **significant other**.  
**Example:** SO to Sophie for taking this picture of me.

### Stan
**Meaning:** A mixture of stalker and fan; someone who is obsessed with a person.  
**Example:** I used to stan him so bad, I don't anymore.

### Stoked
**Meaning:** Very excited.  
**Example:** I was so stoked when he invited me.

### Sus
**Meaning:** Suspicious, suspect. Often used as “slim sus” to mean “a little bit suspicious.” It originates from the game Among Us.  
**Example:** Are you lying or something? You've been acting super sus lately.

### Swerve
**Meaning:** To go out of one's way to avoid someone.  
**Example:** I swerved when I saw my ex.

### Swole
**Meaning:** Having big muscles.  
**Example:** My goal for this year is to get really swole.

### Swoop
**Meaning:** To give someone a ride, or get a ride from someone.  
**Example:** Since your car's in the shop, I'll swoop in 10.

## T

### Take a seat
**Meaning:** A dismissive or sarcastic remark, suggesting that someone should calm down or stop talking.  
**Example:** The troll got out of control, so I told him, “Take a seat.”

### Tea
**Meaning:** Gossiping about something.  
**Example:** Sarah will know about that, just ask her. She loves to spill the tea.

### TFW
**Meaning:** An acronym that stands for **that feeling when**, used to describe an emotionally charged experience.  
**Example:** TFW you cozy up with hot chocolate watching the snowfall outside.

### Thirsty
**Meaning:** Someone who is desperate or eager for attention, or approval.  
**Example:** He followed my Instagram account and immediately liked all my posts. He's so thirsty.

### Thirst Trap
**Meaning:** A sexually provocative photo, video, or comment posted on social media, often with the intention of receiving attention from viewers.  
**Example:** All he posts are thirst traps on TikTok.

### Touch grass
**Meaning:** Used to encourage someone to spend more time in the real world instead of online.  
**Example:** After spending the entire weekend playing video games, his friends told him it was time to touch grass and get some sunlight.

### Twin
**Meaning:** A true friend or friend that is so close that the two share a long list of interests, experiences, or characteristics.  
**Example:** Hey twin, when you comin' over?

### Twizzy
**Meaning:** Variation of **twin**.  
**Example:** Hey twizzy, when you comin' over?

## U

### Unc
**Meaning:** Short for **uncle**, used to refer to a cool millennial uncle or to slightly older people in general.  
**Example:** Just walking up those stairs made me so tired, I think I've reached unc status.

### Understood the assignment
**Meaning:** When someone goes above and beyond.  
**Example:** This was the best birthday ever! You definitely understood the assignment.

## V

### V
**Meaning:** Abbreviation for **very**.  
**Example:** I'm v excited for prom next weekend.

### Vanilla
**Meaning:** Ordinary, boring, lacking special features.  
**Example:** I tried having a conversation with him, but he's so vanilla.

### Vibe
**Meaning:** A feeling, an ambience.  
**Example:** I love your whole vibe.

### VSCO girl
**Meaning:** Pronounced “visco.” A young, usually white, girl who is adorned in trendy things and posts pictures edited on the VSCO app.  
**Example:** She's wearing oversized T-shirts, Birks, and has a huge Hydro Flask. She's definitely trying to be a VSCO girl.

## W

### W
**Meaning:** Win, winner.  
**Example:** Our team took the W.

### Wallflower
**Meaning:** An introvert, someone who avoids the limelight and is more comfortable standing on the sidelines of an activity.  
**Example:** She's always been a wallflower at school dances.

### Whip
**Meaning:** A car, especially one that is stylish.  
**Example:** She just pulled up in her new whip.

### Woke
**Meaning:** Someone who is aware of important facts, and racial and social injustices. It can be used sarcastically.  
**Example:** I used to be ignorant of what was happening around me, but now I'm woke.

### WYA
**Meaning:** An acronym that stands for **where you at?** Commonly used in text messages to ask someone about their current location.  
**Example:** Let me know wya and I'll come meet up.
